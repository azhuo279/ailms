AI Agent Avatar — Design Directions

Flat 2D exploration ahead of the react-three-fiber build. Three directions × active / dormant / transition. All fills use the reserved --color-ai-* ramp only.

Direction 1

Soft Blob Cube

Toy-like and minimal. A single flat pastel tint per shape (ai-200 body, ai-100 face panel), thick rounded outline, no facet shading. Reads friendly and simple at small sizes; the face sits on a subtly inset panel rather than directly on the body.

Active
Face forward, both appendages flank the body
spins
Dormant
Corner-on, face hidden, only one appendage visible
Direction 2

Faceted Isometric

A faceted cube read where light does the work — no drawn outlines anywhere; edges emerge purely from tone contrast between facets (ai-100 top / ai-300 left / ai-200 front). In the active state the cube faces the viewer straight on: the front facet fills most of the frame with the face on it, and only a thin top sliver and a sliver of the left facet peek out to keep the sense of volume. Corners are rounded, but more subtly than directions 1 and 3.

Active
Front-facing — face reads straight on, edges shown only by facet tone
spins
Dormant
Corner leads the view — only top + left facet visible, face and 2nd appendage hidden behind
Direction 3

Glass Gradient Cube

Premium and glowy. A soft diagonal gradient across ai-100 → ai-400 gives volume without hard facet lines, plus a glossy highlight streak in the top-left. Appendages read as small glass orbs-in-cubes that pick up an inner glow, hinting they are "energy" extensions of the body rather than solid limbs.

Active
Gradient + gloss highlight, glowing appendage orbs on both sides
spins
Dormant
Dimmer, corner-on silhouette with a faint inner core glow, one orb visible
